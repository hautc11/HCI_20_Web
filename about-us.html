About us

Hotel L’Appel du Vide

Oscar Comtet, the director, runs it with vitality, always wishing to revisit the place so that it stays ephemeral yet timeless. A place full of history, where thousands stories can be told, but many of which remain to be created.

The Hotel L’Appel du Vide intends to be an establishment welcoming epicureans, where our guests are able to enjoy a variety of offers throughout season, from breakfast to dinner, including brunch in our restaurant Le Grand Salon whilst also keeping in mind the events and the sophisticated cocktails at the Très Particulier, all indoors and on the terraces.
