L'Appel du Vide

The Hotel L’appel du Vide, haven of greenery nestled between the famous Avenue Junot and the Rue Lepic, is the last relic from the scrubland of Montmartre, historical treasure of the Butte.

Restaurant

Hotel L'Appel du Vide restaurant, and its chef Gabriele Faiella, invite you for a culinary experience in a enchangting setting, at any time of the day, from breakfast to dinner, including tea-time, luch, cocktails and bruch.

Bar

Le très particulier is a lively and sensuous place that is sure to surprise as its atmosphere evolves with the passing of time, unveiling an intimate to thriving nightlife.

PARISIAN
HEDONISM

Welcome to the Hotel L’appel du Vide, the most private and smallest Parisian hotels ! A boutique hotel in Paris, this haven of greenery that used to belong to the Hermes family and became a Private House allows you to discover the largest hotel garden in the capital, part of the last vestiges of the Maquis de Montmartre.

Over time this family house that was once called “une Folie” has established itself as a reference in Paris.

Whether for a drink, for a dinner or for a night, the Hotel L’appel du Vide awaits you in Montmartre in what many people rightly call “a countryside in the heart of Paris”.

Infinity Pool

What could be more romantic than relaxing together in the infinity pool? Combine it with a great view and a romantic seafood dinner for two. Sounds good, right? If so, L'Appel du Vide is a right choice for you.
